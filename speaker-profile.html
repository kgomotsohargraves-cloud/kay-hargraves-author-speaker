Biography

Kgomotso "Kay" Hargraves is a global speaker, Christian author, executive financial-crimes specialist and advisor, entrepreneur, lecturer, leadership coach, and daughter of the Most High.

She speaks from Botswana roots, marketplace leadership, boardroom experience, and a deeply personal faith journey that gives language to pressure, identity, fear, courage, integrity, and purpose.

Kay is the author of Quieting the Storm and Unmasking the Spirit of Fear, and publisher of the Anchored Leadership newsletter.

Signature Message

Kay brings biblical principles into practical spaces: the church, the marketplace, the boardroom, and the living room.

Her work helps audiences confront fear, recover peace, lead with clarity, and walk in God-given authority without losing who they are.
