## Master Prompt

Write a powerful, Christ-centered social media announcement for the release of my second book, Unmasking the Spirit of Fear by Kgomotso "Kay" Hargraves. The tone should be heartfelt, warm, bold, grateful, and full of faith. Include that this book comes from my personal journey with fear from a young age, the seeds the enemy planted, the patterns fear created, and the freedom Jesus brings through truth, restoration, and authority. Mention that the book is for anyone who has battled fear, delay, confusion, rejection, spiritual heaviness, or patterns they could not explain.
