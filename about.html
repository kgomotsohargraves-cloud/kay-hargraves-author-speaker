About Kay

Global voice. Marketplace wisdom. Daughter of the Most High.

Kgomotso "Kay" Hargraves speaks from a life tested in real rooms: American financial institutions, African boardrooms, leadership tables, family spaces, and the secret place with God.

The Calling

A life lived, led, refined, and ready to pour out.

Kgomotso "Kay" Hargraves is a global speaker, author, entrepreneur, lecturer, leadership coach, and marketplace voice. She has built authority as an executive financial-crimes specialist and advisor across major American financial institutions, and she has carried governance responsibility as a Vice Chairperson serving in African boardrooms.

But her story is not carried by titles alone. Kay's life has been shaped by faith, pressure, obedience, resilience, spiritual warfare, leadership, motherhood, Botswana roots, marketplace excellence, and the restoring authority of Jesus Christ. She has lived enough to speak with depth, led enough to speak with credibility, and been healed enough to speak with compassion.

Her message is for real places and real people: church rooms, boardrooms, living rooms, corporate engagements, classrooms, podcasts, retreats, small groups, and the private places where someone is asking whether God can meet them in the pressure they carry. Kay brings biblical wisdom into the marketplace without watering down truth and into faith spaces without ignoring real life.

Above every role, accomplishment, platform, and assignment, Kay's most important identity is this: she is a daughter of the Most High. Everything she writes, teaches, builds, and speaks flows from that place.
