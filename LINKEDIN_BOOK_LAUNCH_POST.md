## Primary Post

I am honored to share that my second book, *Unmasking the Spirit of Fear*, is now available.

This book is deeply personal, but it is also deeply practical.

Fear does not only show up in obvious places. It can shape identity, delay purpose, affect decision-making, silence confidence, and quietly follow people into homes, churches, workplaces, leadership rooms, and boardrooms.

I wrote this book from testimony, but also from a life lived in real rooms:

The professional making decisions under weight. The family member trying to break patterns. The person who needs language for what fear planted and truth for what Christ restores.

My prayer is that this book helps readers recognize the lies that shaped patterns, return to biblical truth, and walk in power, love, and a sound mind.
