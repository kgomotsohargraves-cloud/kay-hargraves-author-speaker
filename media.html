Short Bio

Kgomotso "Kay" Hargraves is a global speaker, Christian author, marketplace leader, executive financial-crimes specialist and advisor, entrepreneur, lecturer, leadership coach, and daughter of the Most High.

Long Bio

Kgomotso "Kay" Hargraves writes and speaks from testimony, Botswana roots, faith, executive leadership, and the restoring authority of Jesus Christ. She has built authority as an executive financial-crimes specialist and advisor across major American financial institutions, while also carrying governance perspective as a Vice Chairperson serving in African boardrooms. As an author, entrepreneur, lecturer, and leadership coach, Kay helps readers and audiences apply biblical wisdom to fear, storms, identity, integrity, pressure, courage, purpose, and leadership from the church to the marketplace to the boardroom to the living room. Through her LinkedIn newsletter, Anchored Leadership, Kay speaks to professionals about faith, integrity, courage, pressure, and purpose in the marketplace. Her deepest identity is not a title, but daughter of the Most High.

Marketplace Positioning

Kay is available for corporate engagements, leadership sessions, marketplace conversations, faith-and-work gatherings, women's leadership events, ethics and integrity discussions, podcasts, and conferences. Her approach applies biblical principles to practical challenges such as pressure, decision-making, courage, financial crimes risk, compliance, influence, identity, and purpose.
